MINDANAO
 Being the second largest island group next to Luzon, Mindanao has been
dubbed the country's "Land of Promise" becasue of its promising natural
resources. It is also known worldwide for being the surf capital of
the Philippines, a famous highly-urbanized city, and jaw-dropping beaches.
